What is a KPI?

Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) are the navigational instruments of modern business.

They are quantifiable measurements used to gauge a company's overall long-term performance. KPIs specifically help determine a company's strategic, financial, and operational achievements, especially compared to those of other businesses within the same sector.

    Recertification Process

    Maintain your professional credentials

    Why Recertification?

    To ensure you maintain current knowledge, skills, and align with evolving HR and business trends.

    Recertification Cycle
    Every 3 Years
    Recertification Methods

    Participants can renew their certification through one of the following:

    Option 1
    Continuing Education

    Earn 36 hours of professional involvement via:

    • Attending webinars
    • Completing advanced HR courses
    • Participating in industry conferences
    • Facilitating relevant training
    • Attending any consultancy in relevant field
    Option 2
    Assessment-Based Renewal

    Complete a recertification assessment (Assignment/Case based) that tests understanding of updated HR practices or recent business case studies.
